About Us

Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. We offer catering services to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet their needs. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company. Whether you are here to plan your wedding or to look for an affordable place to stay during your works at the temple, we are here for you, and we have different options that could perfectly meet your needs.

Durban Temple

The temple was announced by President Thomas S. Monson on October 1, 2011. Elder Carl B. Cook, then President of the Africa Southeast Area, presided over the groundbreaking ceremony on April 9, 2016. The Durban South Africa Temple was dedicated on Sunday, February 16, 2020. The temple is located atop a grassy knoll about 12 miles north of the Durban city center.

2 Izinga Drive, Izinga Ridge 4021, Umhlanga, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa

Johannesburg Temple

Plans to construct the temple were announced 1 April 1981, Church leaders broke ground 27 November 1982, and President Gordon B. Hinckley, then a counselor in the First Presidency of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, dedicated the Johannesburg South Africa Temple 24 August 1985. The temple serves Latter-day Saints in southern Africa.

7 Jubilee Road, Parktown, Johannesburg 2193, South Africa
