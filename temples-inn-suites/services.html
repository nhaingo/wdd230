Our Services

Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. We offer catering services to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company. Whether you are here to look for an appropriate place to stay for few days, to plan your wedding or to look for an affordable place to live during your mission at the temple as a full time temple missionary, we are here for you, and we have different options that could perfectly meet your needs.
