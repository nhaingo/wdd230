Discover the District of Ambohidratrimo

Ambohidratrimo is one of the districts of Antananarivo, the capital of Madagascar. It is a booming area with impressive places to discover. Considered a rural district, it is located 15 km north west of the capital. GreenFinger's aims to promote the district where the chamber's office is currently based.

Ravinala Airport

The airport of Ravinala is the largest and the first international airport of Madagascar. It is located in the district of Ambohidratrimo. In addition to being the gateway to the country, it is a showcase of Malagasy flora and culture, at the service of the economic and tourist development of Madagascar. It takes its name from an endemic plant that belongs only to the country.

The Fortress of Ambohidratrimo

The Fortress of Ambohidratrimo is one of the twelve sacred hills of Antananarivo. It is a sacred place, where the remains of the past are well preserved in a royal palace called Rova d'Ambohidratrimo, and where the royal tombs, topped with the traditional 'Tranomanara' in wood, tell the amazing stories of the buried royalty.

La ferme d’Ivato (Ivato Farm)

La Ferme d'Ivato is located close to Ivato International Airport. Besides growing fruits and vegetables, the farm is also raising chickens, ducks and rabbits. For day or overnight visitors there is a bungalow, restaurant, cafe and a mountain bike hire service. The business is part of Le Combava, a hotel between the airport and the capital.

Croc Farm

Croc Farm is a zoological and botanical park located in a small valley following a bumpy 10-minute drive from Ivato Airport. Visitors can observe more than 80 animal species and endemic plants and flowers. The smallest chameleon in the world, free-living Sifaka lemurs, fossa, snakes, frogs, parrots and turtoises, as well as hundreds of crocodiles can be seen at the park.

La Hutte Canadienne

La Hutte Canadienne is a private company located at a 2 minutes’ drive from the international airport of Ivato. Its business is centered around food retailing, food products, farming, agriculture, and industrial equipment activities.

Aerovillage

Aerovillage is a commercial center, based in the municipality of Talatamaty, which is part of Ambohidratrimo district. It is located at 5 minutes’ drive from the airport. The place hosts several shopping center like a store, a food court, a hair salon, a cheese store, as well as one of the emblematic chocolate store of the country. Banks and insurance company can also be found at this place.

The municipality of Talatamaty

Talatamaty is a well-known area. It is one of the municipalities inside the district of Ambohidratrimo, and one of the largest. It is located at 10km north-west of the capital city Antananarivo. Talatamaty is a fast-growing area and is a very diverse place in term of business.

Marina Casino and Hotel

Marina Casino is a casino and hotel, located at 5 minutes drive from the international airport. It is part of the Golden Peacock Hotel, in the municipality of Talatamaty. The hotel is one of the exotic hotels in Madagascar featuring a variety of amenities that include spacious guest rooms, fitness center, conference room, spa and an outdoor pool.

The Mining Business Center

Mining Business Center is located on the main road of Ivato airport. The multi-faceted design concept of the exterior wall comes from the diamond itself, known as the diamond curtain wall. The complex structure and shape of the project fully reflect the technical strength of the company, allowing it to become an iconic building in Madagascar.
