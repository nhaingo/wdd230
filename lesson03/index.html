Overview

Purpose

Green'Fingers Chamber aims to promote the local agriculture industry, and to strengthen the networks and the expertise of the entities. It also focuses on attracting investors and to develop the tourism in the district. This site will serve as a portal for the agribusiness of the area. The main objective of Green'Fingers Chamber is to support the development of small farmers' cooperatives and agricultural SMEs by providing comprehensive information, training and advisory services. Other services are available at the request of the chamber's clients or based on the need to provide such services through co-creation. Our training activities are carried out throughout the district through the direct implementation of projects and partnerships to improve local farmers' agricultural production, market linkages and sustainable incomes. Important local, regional and international events that could be related to its development and that could benefit each member will be updated here on a regular basis.
