Overview

Purpose

Green'Fingers Chamber is focusing its activity on the development of agriculture industry in the municipality of Talatamaty, a local town located 5km away from the International Airport of Madagascar. Its purpose is to bring together local farmers, technicians and operators, as well as all those interested in promoting the business around it. The primary goal of the chamber is to promote the local agriculture industry, and to strengthen the networks and the expertise of the entities. It is also aimed to attract investors and to develop the tourism in the city. This site will serve as a portal for the agribusiness of the municipality. Important local, regional and international events that could be related to its development and that could benefit each member will be updated here on a regular basis.
